            <h2>About Me</h2>
            <div class="about-text">
                <p>I am currently a Researcher Scientist at the School of Physical Sciences, Great Bay University, China. I received my Ph.D. degree in Materials Science and Engineering from China University of Geosciences (Beijing) in 2020. I previously worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Microscale Optoelectronics, Shenzhen University (2022–2025), and as an Assistant Researcher at Chongqing University (2020–2022). I also gained research experience as a trainee at the Photocatalyst Research Group, National Institute for Materials Science (NIMS), Japan (2018–2020).</p>
                <p>My research interest focuses on <strong>catalytic materials design for photoredox-mediated value-added chemical synthesis</strong>, with particular emphasis on photocatalytic H<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub> production and organic transformation. I have published multiple peer-reviewed papers in high-impact journals including <em>Applied Catalysis B: Environmental</em>, <em>Acta Physico-Chimica Sinica</em>, and <em>Applied Surface Science</em>. I am the Principal Investigator of a Young Scientists Fund project from the National Natural Science Foundation of China (2025–2027). I have received several honors, including the National Scholarship of China (2016) and multiple academic achievement awards.</p>
